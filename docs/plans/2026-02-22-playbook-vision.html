02 / How It Works

Six Steps, One Continuous Loop

The full journey from zero to compounding intelligence. Each step feeds the next. The loop never stops -- it just gets tighter and faster.

🔍
Step 1
Research
AI as Analyst

AI scrapes your website, researches your market, analyzes competitors, and maps your space. You answer 3-5 key questions about your business. The AI does the rest -- coming back with findings, not more questions.

Try Phase
✍️
Step 2
Strategize
AI as GTM Consultant

AI builds your GTM playbook: ideal customer profile, buyer personas, value propositions, channel strategy, messaging framework. You review, refine, and approve. The AI challenges weak assumptions and fills gaps proactively.

Try Phase
👥
Step 3
Source
AI as Sourcing Assistant

AI finds contacts matching your ICP. Filters by intent signals -- hiring patterns, growth indicators, tech stack clues. Enriches with company data from real websites. You select who to reach and who to skip.

Run Phase
✉️
Step 4
Engage
AI as Messaging Coach

AI generates personalized messages for each persona. Calibrates tone, depth, and framing per segment. You review, tweak, and approve. A/B variants launch automatically. Campaigns go out across your chosen channels.

Run Phase
📈
Step 5
Measure
AI as Performance Analyst

Track open rates, reply rates, meeting rates, pipeline generated. AI surfaces what is working and what fell flat. Not just metrics -- narratives. "ROI framing outperformed innovation framing 3:1 for manufacturing CTOs in DACH."

Evaluate Phase
🧠
Step 6
Learn
AI as Strategist-in-Residence

AI updates your strategy based on real results. Refines ICP scoring. Adjusts messaging frameworks. Reprioritizes channels. The next campaign starts where the last one left off -- not from scratch. The loop closes. Cycle 2 begins smarter than cycle 1.

04 / For the AI-First Founder

You're the CEO, Not the Operator

You're building the future of work while living the loneliest version of it. Your AI strategist should feel like the first real hire you made -- not another dashboard to check.

"You're building the future of work while living the loneliest version of it. Your AI strategist doesn't just execute -- it thinks alongside you."

-- The design principle behind every interaction

🎤
Voice Dialog

Strategize on your commute. Review contacts while walking the dog. Approve messages over morning coffee. Your hands never need to touch a keyboard.

🧑‍💻
AI Avatar

Not text in a chat box -- a face. An animated teammate that presents findings, reacts to your decisions, and makes the room feel less empty.

🌐
Language-Aware

When you switch to Czech mid-sentence because English can't capture the nuance, the AI switches too. No friction, no awkwardness.

💥
Frustration Detection

Detects tone shifts, terse replies, and signs of fatigue. Adapts immediately -- shorter responses, a different angle, or the suggestion to take a break.

🔔
Proactive Check-Ins

Your AI strategist checks in with progress, asks for decisions at the right moments, and nudges when something needs your attention -- without being annoying.

💡
Decision-Ready Briefings

No raw data, no 50-slide decks. Just "Here's what I found, here's what I recommend, here's what I need from you." Three minutes, max.
