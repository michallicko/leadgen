# Test Subject Profile: United Arts & Co.

## Company Profile

### Basic Info
- **Company**: United Arts & Co. z.s. (originally United Arts s.r.o., restructured 2015)
- **Website**: https://unitedarts.cz
- **Location**: Prague, Czech Republic
- **Industry**: Entertainment / Event Production / Contemporary Circus Arts
- **Legal form**: z.s. (registered association) for artistic operations; commercial activities separated into a sibling entity

### What They Do
United Arts is a professional production and event agency specializing in entertainment services for corporate and private events. They provide:

1. **Composed Programs** ("Komponovane programy") -- Custom shows featuring choreography, direction, scenography, music, lighting design, costumes, and full technical production, tailored to event concepts
2. **Artist Booking** -- Individual performers and acrobatic groups available for hire (aerial silks, strength acrobatics, circus acts, visual poi, roller skate shows, cyr wheel, slack line, living statues, stilt walkers)
3. **Full-Evening Presentations** -- Complete theatrical shows bookable at their venue (Divadlo BRAVO! in Prague's Branik) or at client locations, with catering and supplementary programming options

### Origin Story
Founded by the duo **DaeMen** -- two performers who won the first season of "Cesko Slovensko ma talent" (Czech-Slovak Got Talent). They expanded through the absurdist cabaret "Lidoskop," then collaborated with more acrobats and dancers to create "The Loser(s)" and eventually **Losers Cirque Company**, a contemporary circus ensemble touring Czech and international venues.

### Associated Acts & Performers
- **Losers Cirque Company** -- Contemporary circus ensemble, Thalia-nominated, full-length touring shows
- **DaeMen** -- The founding duo (now appears only for special occasions)
- **Duo React** -- Frequently booked acrobatic duo (appears in ~10 client bookings in the contact list)
- **Duo Elevate** -- Acrobatic duo
- **Josefina (+ pianist)** -- Solo performer with piano accompaniment (frequently booked)
- **Vanda** -- Aerial hoop specialist
- **Roxane** -- Performer (mentioned in Obecni dum event)
- **Losers Academy** -- Regular partner acrobatics and gymnastics classes

### Venue
- **Divadlo BRAVO!** -- Home theater in Prague's Branicky divadel (Branik district), with extensive performance history

### Target Market
Corporate event organizers and agencies that book entertainment for:
- Corporate galas and holiday parties
- Product launches and brand events
- Conferences and congresses
- Award ceremonies
- Festival programming
- Municipal/cultural events

### Reference Clients (from website)
Microsoft, Skoda, O2, Mercedes-Benz, CSOB, Bosch

### Value Proposition
"Unforgettable audiovisual experiences" through collaboration with experienced performers from various artistic disciplines, combined with professional technical production. They handle everything from concept to execution -- choreography, scenography, lighting, costumes, music, and technical rigging.

### Artistic Philosophy
Innovative contemporary circus arts combined with theatrical elements, blending acrobatic skill with creative storytelling and visual design. Not traditional circus -- modern, artistic, visually striking performances.
